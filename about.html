About Lion Claw Safaris

Your trusted partner in African safari adventures

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lion Claw Safaris has been at the forefront of providing exceptional safari experiences across East Africa. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the beauty and wonder of Africa's wilderness with travelers from around the world.

Over the years, we've grown from a small local operator to a trusted name in safari tourism, while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and authentic experiences.

Our Values

Passion for Africa

We are deeply passionate about Africa's wildlife and landscapes, and we love sharing this passion with our guests.

Trust & Reliability

We build lasting relationships with our clients through transparency, honesty, and consistent service delivery.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible tourism practices that protect Africa's natural heritage for future generations.

Community Focus

We actively support local communities and contribute to their development through our tourism activities.
