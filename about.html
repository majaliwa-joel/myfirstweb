Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lion Claw Safaris has been at the forefront of providing exceptional safari experiences in Tanzania. Our journey began with a simple mission: to connect people with the raw beauty of Africa while ensuring sustainable tourism practices.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing authentic safari experiences that respect wildlife and local communities. Our goal is to create unforgettable memories while promoting conservation and sustainable tourism.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced guides, wildlife experts, and passionate professionals who have dedicated their lives to understanding and preserving Africa's natural wonders. Each member brings unique expertise and local knowledge to ensure the best possible safari experience.

Why Choose Us

  • Expert local guides with years of experience
  • Commitment to sustainable tourism
  • Personalized safari experiences
  • Small group sizes for better wildlife viewing
  • Comfortable and well-maintained vehicles
  • Strong focus on safety and customer satisfaction
